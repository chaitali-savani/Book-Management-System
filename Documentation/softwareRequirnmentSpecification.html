Software Requirements Specification: E-Book Store

1. Introduction

The Software Requirements Specification (SRS) outlines the requirements for the development of an E-Book Store system, which allows users to browse, search, and purchase e-books online.

2. Functional Requirements

2.1 User Registration and Authentication

  • Users shall be able to register for an account with their name, email, and password.
  • Registered users shall be able to log in securely to their accounts.

2.2 Browse and Search E-Books

  • Users shall be able to browse e-books by categories or genres.
  • Users shall be able to search for e-books by title, author, or keyword.

2.3 Purchase E-Books

  • Users shall be able to view detailed information about e-books, including price and description.
  • Users shall be able to add e-books to their shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Users shall be able to make secure payments for purchased e-books.

2.4 Manage User Account

  • Users shall be able to view and update their profile information.
  • Users shall be able to view their purchase history.

3. Non-Functional Requirements

3.1 Security

  • The system shall ensure the security of user data and transactions through encryption.
  • The system shall implement secure authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.

3.2 Performance

  • The system shall be responsive and provide fast loading times for browsing and searching e-books.
  • The system shall be able to handle multiple concurrent user sessions efficiently.

3.3 Compatibility

  • The system shall be compatible with popular web browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.
  • The system shall be responsive and accessible on different devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

4. Glossary

Users: Individuals who browse, search, and purchase e-books through the system.
Admins: Administrators who manage the e-book store, including adding new e-books and managing user accounts.
E-Books: Electronic books available for purchase through the system.
